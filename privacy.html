Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2025

1. Who we are

This website is operated by Opsistech, a technology and engineering firm based in Romania. You can contact us at support@opsistech.ro.

2. What data we collect

We only collect personal data that you voluntarily provide through our contact form:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The message you submit

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any analytics tools. We do not collect data passively or automatically.

3. Why we collect it

We collect this data solely to respond to your inquiry. The legal basis for processing is your consent, given at the time of submitting the form (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR).

4. How we use it

Your data is used only to reply to your message. We do not use it for marketing, do not sell it, and do not share it with third parties except as described below.

5. Third-party processors

We use the following third-party services to operate this website:

  • EmailJS (emailjs.com) — processes form submissions to deliver your message to us. EmailJS is GDPR compliant. Data is transmitted securely and not stored beyond delivery.
  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) — serves typefaces used on this site. Your browser may connect to Google's servers to load fonts. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts this website. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.

6. Data retention

We retain your contact data only for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry, and no longer than 12 months unless a business relationship requires otherwise.

7. Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Romanian data protection authority (ANSPDCP) at dataprotection.ro

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@opsistech.ro.

8. Security

All data transmitted through our contact form is sent over HTTPS. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your personal data.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
